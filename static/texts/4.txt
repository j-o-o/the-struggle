<h1>Angel Genkov - MASKS ARE USED TO KEEP YOU SHUT, NOT TO KEEP YOU SAFE</h1>
<p>
„Corona“ – this is how it all started. Turned into a pandemic situation and led to keeping
</p>
<p>
millions of people stay inside their homes. <br>
What a better way to rethink our lives and adapt to the current situation. Of course, it’s possible. <br>
We stay at home, do what they tell us, follow their rules, listen to their words, but somehow, we are not able to speak. <br>
We are somewhat muted. It’s political, yes. The series follow the daily life of a person, who is properly protected. <br>
However, being properly protected, speaking and listening is not quite possible, so you better stay home, where your voice can be heard.<br>
</p>
<p>
The inspiration came from a tragic incident of a Bulgarian reporter, killed in a car crash by drugged teenager, who decided to go outside and choose the “normal” life, instead of being protected. <br>
Just a few minutes before the incident the reported posted a photo with a mask and the text: “Masks are used to keep you shut, not to keep you safe.”
</p>
