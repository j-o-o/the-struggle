<h1>LOST IN 24 PARALLELS </h1>

<p>
This is the story of “ Character X ”. <br>
The deep reflection on different points of their journey and the situations that they ended up in is chronologically presented in this series . <br>
Throughout the story, X reflects on the impact of “daddy” in their life, who has little- to-no existence anymore . <br>
However, X still realizes the ontological impact of it on their lifedecisions. <br>
To X, a father figure was always linked to being a source of money (truthfully, not much more) . <br>
Later on, X is presented with an opportunity to introduce other daddy figures. <br>
However, little to their conscious knowledge, this experience came with consequences. <br>
The series ends with X’s form transferring back to a tunnel that manifests the start of their journey. <br>
As feelings of loss and confusion remain in the back of their conscious mind, different forms of satisfaction and inner peace crawl in to X’s mind to create a new state of existence. 
</p>
